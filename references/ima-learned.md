## 1. Li Lu's Value Investment Philosophy (李录投资哲学)

**Source**: IMA KB article "李录投资哲学与生涯决策全景分析：从芒格门徒到中国价值投资巨擘"

### Core Principles

| Principle | Description | Application to StockMasterHunter |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------------------|
| Civilization-Scale Perspective | Place investments in the context of human civilization development. Identify structural societal transformations. | Enhances L (Longevity) scoring — industries riding multi-decade civilization shifts score higher |
| Multi-Disciplinary Integration | Synthesize history, biology, psychology, economics — not just financial analysis | Broadens Q (Quality) assessment beyond financial metrics |
| Concentrated Conviction | Hold < 10 positions with multi-year minimum holding periods | Reinforces the importance of high SQGLP composite scores — only the best candidates merit capital |
| Compounding Moat | The best businesses widen their moat over decades through network effects, data flywheels, scale | Q scoring should assess moat trajectory (widening/narrowing), not just current state |
| Circle of Competence | Only invest in deeply understood businesses | Boundary rule: if business model is complex/unclear, score S/Q/G/L/P conservatively |

### Key Case Studies
- BYD (2008 entry, held through multiple cycles)
- Kweichow Moutai (pricing power moat)
- Pinduoduo (network effects + data flywheel)

## 2. Buffett Trade Timing Framework (巴菲特买入时机)

**Source**: IMA KB article "从巴菲特的历史交易来看买入的机会"

### Key Insights

| Period | Investment | Crisis Context | Entry Discount | Holding Period |
|--------|-----------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| 1976 | GEICO | Insurance industry crisis | ~80% below prior peak | 20+ years |
| 1990 | Wells Fargo | S&L Crisis, real estate panic | ~50% discount | 25+ years |
| 2008-2011 | Bank of America | Financial crisis | Preferred + warrants | 10+ years |
| 2016 | Apple | Perceived "peak iPhone" narrative | ~12x PE at entry | 10+ years |

### Application Rules
1. **Crisis Entry Premium**: When a SQGLP score > 7.0 but the stock is in a Wave A/C correction (down 40%+ from peak), this is the highest-conviction entry zone per Buffett's framework.
2. **"Too Hard" Pile**: If the investment thesis requires more than 3 paragraphs to explain, move to the "too hard" pile.
3. **Intrinsic Value Gap**: Calculate rough intrinsic value (DCF or earnings power value). If market price < 60% of intrinsic value AND SQGLP > 7.0, flag as "Buffett Zone."

## 3. PAG Control-Investment Philosophy (单伟建控股投资)

**Source**: IMA KB article "单伟建与PAG太盟投资：困境猎手的资本哲学"

### Framework

```
Traditional Value Investing:  Buy undervalued → Wait for revaluation → Sell
PAG Control Investing:        Buy control → Operationally transform → Value creation → Exit
```

### Key Deal: Wanda Commercial (万达商管)

| Phase | Year | Action | Value Created |
|-------|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | 2021 | $2.8B Pre-IPO investment | Entry at distressed valuation |
| 2 | 2024 | ¥60B for 60% control of Dalian New Wanda | Control premium capture |
| 3 | 2025 | Acquisition of 48 core-city Wanda Plazas | Asset-level value unlock |

### Application to Turnaround Stocks
For stocks scoring low on G (Growth) but high on S (Size) and L (Longevity tailwinds):
- **PAG Catalyst Score**: 1-5 rating of whether a catalyst (management change, restructuring, asset sale) can unlock value.
- Turnaround 10-baggers often emerge when PAG Catalyst Score > 3 AND the stock is trading near Wave C bottom.

## 5. DBS (Danaher Business System) Operations Framework

**Source**: IMA KB article "丹纳赫（Danaher）业务系统（DBS）的核心技能体系"

### DBS 8-Skill Framework

| Skill | Category | Relevance to Q (Quality) Scoring |
|-------|----------|----------------------------------|
| VSM & Waste Identification | Lean | Operating efficiency → margin expansion |
| Standard Work | Lean | Process consistency → earnings predictability |
| Kaizen (Continuous Improvement) | Lean | Culture of improvement → compounding operational gains |
| Problem Solving Process (PSP) | Growth | Root cause analysis → sustainable problem resolution |
| Voice of Customer (VOC) | Growth | Market-driven innovation → revenue durability |
| Policy Deployment (Hoshin Kanri) | Leadership | Strategy-to-execution alignment |
| Talent Development | Leadership | Human capital → moat sustainability |
| Transaction & Integration | Growth | M&A capability → inorganic growth engine |

### DBS Score (0-8)
Count how many of the 8 DBS skills a company demonstrates:
- **7-8**: World-class operations → Add +1 to Q score
- **5-6**: Strong operations → Add +0.5 to Q score
- **3-4**: Average → No adjustment
- **0-2**: Operational weakness → Subtract -1 from Q score
