## Lessons Learned

1. **The Wave 3 primary advance is the wealth-creation engine.** In 603256's case, Wave 3 accounted for >80% of the total 40x return. Missing Wave 2's bottom was forgivable. Missing Wave 3's launch was catastrophic.

2. **SQGLP composite > 7.0 with a structural industry tailwind is a high-conviction signal.** The combination of small size (S=9), high growth (G=8), and long-duration tailwinds (L=8) created a compounding flywheel.

3. **The O'Dea Volume Principle correctly filtered both fakeouts.** The +20% volume threshold on breakouts and the -8% contraction rule on retests provided accurate entry/stop-loss signals throughout the cycle.

4. **Bearish MACD divergence at the Wave 5 blow-off was the definitive exit signal.** Price made a marginal new high, but MACD made a lower high. Those who exited on divergence preserved 35-40x gains.

5. **Sideways Wave 4s are accumulation zones, not exit zones.** The alternation rule (sharp W2 → sideways W4) signaled that the trend was intact and a Wave 5 advance was likely.
